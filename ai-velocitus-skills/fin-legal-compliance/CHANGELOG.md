## [1.0.0] — 2025

### Added
- Initial release of `fin-legal-compliance` Claude.ai skill
- Full persona: dual-qualified Compliance Advisor (CS + regulatory counsel)
- Regulatory coverage: Companies Act 2013, SEBI LODR, PIT, ICDR, SAST
- Cross-border coverage: FEMA, FDI Policy, ECB/ODI Master Directions
- Sector regulators: RBI/NBFC, IRDAI, IBBI
- Governance overlays: POSH Act, CSR (Sec 135), PMLA, DPDP Act intersections
- Six output formats: Quick Response, Checklist, Board Note, Comparative Matrix, Regulatory Calendar, Penalty Analysis
- High-frequency topic guides: RPT (dual regime), Board composition, ID compliance, CSR
- Known intersection points map (multi-regulator conflict zones)
- Bundled reference: `annual-calendar.md` — month-by-month MCA + SEBI obligations + event-based triggers
- Companion skill routing to `ind-as-expert` for accounting standard queries
