## Event-Based Triggers (Non-Calendar)

| Event | Obligation | Deadline | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Director appointment | DIR-12 filing | 30 days | Sec 170 |
| Director resignation | DIR-12 + disclosure | 30 days | Sec 168 / LODR |
| Change in KMP | Board resolution + MCA filing | 30 days | Sec 203 |
| UPSI creation | Record in SDD; trading window closure | Immediately | PIT Reg 9A |
| Material event (merger, court order, default) | Disclosure to stock exchange | 24 hours | LODR Reg 30 |
| RPT above threshold | AC approval before transaction | Prior | LODR Reg 23 |
| FDI received | FC-GPR filing | 30 days from allotment | FEMA / RBI |
| Overseas investment made | Form ODI-Part I | Prior or within 30 days | FEMA / RBI |
| ECB drawdown | Form ECB-2 | Monthly reporting | RBI |
| Charge creation | CHG-1 | 30 days (extendable to 60) | Sec 77 |
| Charge satisfaction | CHG-4 | 30 days | Sec 82 |
| Increase in authorized capital | SH-7 + board + shareholder resolution | Before allotment | Sec 61 |
| Buy-back | Sec 68 SPA + SEBI Buyback Regs | Per regulatory timeline | MCA / SEBI |

---

*Note: Deadlines above are for standard cases. Always verify current position as MCA/SEBI may defer or amend timelines through circulars.*
